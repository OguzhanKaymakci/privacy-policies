WeatherFit ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights as a user. By using WeatherFit, you agree to the practices described below.

1

Information We Collect

WeatherFit is designed with privacy in mind. Most data never leaves your device. The following information may be stored locally or processed during app use:

Stored on your device only
  • City or location information (for weather lookup)
  • Notification preferences (enabled/disabled, time)
  • Language preference
  • Gender and clothing style selections
None of the above personal preferences are transmitted to our servers. They exist solely on your device to personalize your experience.
2

Location Data

WeatherFit requests access to your device's location in order to provide accurate, real-time weather information for your area.

  • Location is only accessed when you open the app or enable automatic updates
  • We do not store your location history on any server
  • Your coordinates or city name are sent to Open-Meteo API solely to retrieve weather data
  • You may manually enter a city instead of granting location permission
Location data is shared with Open-Meteo solely for weather retrieval purposes. Open-Meteo is a free and open-source weather API and does not use your location for advertising or profiling. See their policy at open-meteo.com/en/terms.
3

Notifications

WeatherFit may send you daily push notifications with outfit suggestions based on the weather forecast for your location.

  • Notifications are opt-in — you will be asked for permission before they are enabled
  • You can disable notifications at any time from the app's Settings screen
  • You can also manage notification permissions via your device's system settings
  • We do not send promotional or marketing notifications
4

Advertising

WeatherFit uses Google AdMob to display advertisements within the app. AdMob may collect and process certain data to serve personalized or non-personalized ads.

AdMob operates under Google's own privacy policy. We do not control what data AdMob collects. Please review Google's privacy policy at policies.google.com/privacy.

Data that AdMob may collect includes device identifiers, IP address, and interaction data with ads. You can opt out of personalized advertising through your device settings:

  • Android: Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization
  • iOS: Settings → Privacy → Tracking → disable for WeatherFit

Children's Privacy

WeatherFit is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through our app, please contact us immediately and we will take steps to remove such information.

7

Your Rights

Since WeatherFit stores preferences only on your device, you have full control over your data:

  • Clear all app data via your device's app settings at any time
  • Revoke location permission from system settings
  • Disable notifications from system settings or in-app settings
  • Opt out of personalized ads through your device's advertising settings

We reserve the right to update this Privacy Policy. If significant changes are made, we will notify you via an in-app notice. Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
