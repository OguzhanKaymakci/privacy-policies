Privacy Policy

PARKSAT  ·  Last updated: April 15, 2025

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy applies to the mobile application PARKSAT ("App"), developed and maintained by Oğuzhan Kaymakçı as an individual developer. By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

2. Data We Collect

The App collects the following data to provide its core functionality:

Data Type Purpose
Location To create parking listings and display them on the map near you
IBAN (bank account number) Shown to the other party upon acceptance of a parking offer for payment purposes
Anonymous user ID Automatically generated by Firebase to identify sessions without personal identity
Device information Automatically collected by Google AdMob to display personalized or non-personalized ads

3. How We Use Your Data

  • Location — used to show your listing on the map and match nearby users with available parking spots.
  • IBAN — displayed only to the matched party after a parking offer is accepted, for payment coordination.
  • Anonymous user ID — used internally by Firebase for session management and push notifications.
  • Device information — used by Google AdMob to serve relevant advertisements within the App.

4. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services, each with their own privacy practices:

  • Google Firebase — data storage, authentication, and push notifications. Privacy Policy
  • Google AdMob — in-app advertising; automatically collects device identifiers and usage data. Privacy Policy
  • Google Maps — map display and location visualization. Privacy Policy

Because the App uses AdMob, Google may collect device identifiers and share data for advertising purposes. You can opt out of personalized ads through your device settings.

5. Data Retention

  • Parking listings automatically expire after 30 minutes and are removed from view.
  • Firestore data associated with expired or deleted listings is removed promptly after expiration.
  • IBAN information is stored only as long as the associated listing is active.

6. Your Rights

  • Data deletion — you may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us via the email below.
  • Uninstalling the App — removing the App from your device stops all local data collection. Server-side data (Firebase) can be deleted upon request.

7. Age Restriction

PARKSAT is intended for users aged 16 and older. The App is not directed at children and does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 16.

8. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with a revised effective date. Continued use of the App after updates constitutes acceptance of the new policy.
